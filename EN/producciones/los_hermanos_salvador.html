The Salvador Siblings

SYNOPSIS

The Salvador siblings follows five siblings as they attempt to recover the lost glory of their unique antique shop. The fate of the family legacy depends on the ability of the quintet of Salvador siblings to put aside their differences, learn to work as a team, and thus restore the forgotten charm of that intriguing antique shop that holds much more than objects from times gone by. At the same time, they must deal with the adventures of the Quiroga siblings, a very unusual family whose mission is to put an end to the Salvador shop forever, unleashing a series of fun and tangled supernatural adventures.

Data sheet:

Filmed in Mexico, the series is produced by Sula Films and distributed by Alebrije Entertainment. It is part of the robust lineup of new productions available on Disney+, aligned with The Walt Disney Company Latin America's commitment to present locally relevant content for the streaming service in partnership with regional production houses and by recruiting local talent.
