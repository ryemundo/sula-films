Benefits of producing in Mexico

Mexico is an ideal platform for production, as it has 9 of the 11 ecosystems in the world. It also has facilities capable of supporting large-scale film and television productions, as well as trained technical and professional staff.

Incentives offered by the country:

0% rate. When exporting film and TV services from Mexico, you can claim the 0% rate, which consists of recovering the tax paid on the services in Mexico (16%).

ATA Carnet, a document that allows the temporary importation of professional equipment for a limited period of time in the country. It basically covers camera equipment, stage machinery, electrical equipment, sound equipment, decoration, props, and costumes.

Production costs below the world average.
